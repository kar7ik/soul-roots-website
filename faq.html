Frequently Asked Questions

What level of yoga experience do I need?

Our retreats are open to all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Our instructors will guide you based on your experience.

What is included in the retreat price?

Retreat prices include accommodation, meals, yoga classes, workshops, and excursions. Flights are not included.

How do I book a retreat?

Visit our Booking page to reserve your spot.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely! Many of our guests come solo and leave with new friends.
